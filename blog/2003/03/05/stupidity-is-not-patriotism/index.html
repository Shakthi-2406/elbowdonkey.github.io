Stupidity Is Not Patriotism

Man gets arrested

for wearing “Give Peace a Chance” shirt. It’s little things like this that get me thinking. If we go to war, protests aren’t going to be pretty, here have a flower type of events. You’re going to have the entire gamut of emotions and opinions, from those who are protesting or supporting the war peacefully, to those who are willing to be total stupid idiots (like the security guards and police in the story above). I think that because of the emotions tied up in this, it’s going to get real messy, with real idiots acting from both ends of the spectrum.
