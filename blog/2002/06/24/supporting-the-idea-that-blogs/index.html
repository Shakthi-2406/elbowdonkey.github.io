Supporting the Idea That Blogs

Supporting the idea that Blogs help hold the promise of the Internet, by chance, I found the blog of a very talented and determined young woman who worked for Price.com. I was thinking about Google’s catalog search feature, and was wondering if Google had any competition in this category when I came across Anna

‘s resume. Anna worked for eCatalogs after Price.com.


I’m often curious about what happened to the crew at Price.com after it downsized, and I’ve kept in touch with as many people as I can, but lost track of Anna. I’m glad to have found her, as her blog is well worth the read.
