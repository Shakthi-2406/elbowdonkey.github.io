On Writing Well

I’ve been reading the second editon of William Zinsser’s “On writing well”. I originally got the book for a project, but I’m finding out it has more benefits than I expected. It’s an informal guide to writing nonfiction. I find myself writing nonfiction almost everyday now. This book is helping refine my writing skills, and I’d recommend it to anyone, writers and nonwriters alike.
