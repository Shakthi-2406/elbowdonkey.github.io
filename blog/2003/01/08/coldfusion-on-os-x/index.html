ColdFusion on OS X

Macromedia has released a couple of new products

that run on OS X, including ColdFusion MX. I always said “If I could get CF to run on a Mac I might switch over.” Well, I haven’t fully switched yet, although Windows is getting uglier and more buggy feeling every day. I still intend to work on both platforms for a couple of reasons, foremost is the fact that I can test web creations on most all of the popular browsers and platforms, but also, just having a healthy dose of each platform daily gives me a broader understanding of software design, and what works well and what doesn’t.
