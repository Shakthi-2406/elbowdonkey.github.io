I Made My First Flash

I made my first Flash 5 driven form tonight that actually submits data to a ColdFusion script (where it could then be stored in a database). I remember this feeling from when I was first discovering ColdFusion. What seems like a complete mystery begins to gel, and with time becomes more clear. Right now things are a bit muddy. I know the ColdFusion side of the process, but the Flash side is muddy. It will all make sense soon. Flash 5 is a component of a top secret project I’ve been conceptualizing for the past 6 months. I can’t wait until I can pull it off, it will be an awesome accomplishment.
